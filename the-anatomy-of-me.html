About

Chief Complaint

A little bit about who I am, where I come from, and what makes me, me.

Hi! I'm Ahana, a high school student who's been fascinated by the human body since I was old enough to ask "but why?" about everything.

I'm drawn to medicine because it sits at the intersection of science and humanity. I want to be the kind of doctor who actually listens, one who sees a whole person, not just a set of symptoms. Outside of studying, I love collecting cute stationery, rewatching comfort shows, and spending too long on aesthetics.

Currently navigating the beautifully chaotic journey of high school life, one flashcard at a time. 🌸
